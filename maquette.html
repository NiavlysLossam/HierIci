Era: La Belle Époque

Quai du Louvre, Automne

calendar_today 14 Octobre 1898location_on 1er Arrondissement

"The city breathed with a different rhythm then. You could hear the clicking of horse hooves against the wet cobblestones long before you saw the carriage emerge from the morning fog."

This rare plate captures the Seine at its most industrious. Notice the small steam barges unloading stone for the construction of the Grand Palais. At this moment, Paris was undergoing a radical transformation under the guidance of the Third Republic, blending medieval charm with modern iron industrialism.
